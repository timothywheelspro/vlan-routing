## ğŸ¯ Pro Tips for Saving Confirmations

### Screenshot Tips:
- Capture the entire confirmation page (not just part)
- Include date/time in screenshot if possible
- Take 2 screenshots: cancellation page + confirmation page
- Make sure reference/confirmation number is visible

### Email Tips:
- Forward confirmation to a dedicated email address (yourself)
- Use email subject: "CANCELED: [Service Name] [Date]"
- Add to calendar: "Verify [Service] cancellation" (30 days out)
- Create email filter: Auto-label subscription cancellation emails

### Backup Tips:
- Save important confirmations in 2 places (email + screenshots)
- Print critical ones if needed (for expensive services)
- Keep records for at least 1 year after cancellation
- Note: Some services may try to charge again months later
