## üí° PRO TIPS

1. **Be Thorough**: Check ALL accounts - subscriptions hide everywhere
2. **Don't Skip Small Ones**: $5/month = $60/year adds up
3. **Check Expired Too**: Some "expired" subscriptions still charge
4. **Look for Annual Charges**: Check full year of statements for annual subscriptions
5. **Investigate Unknown Charges**: If you see a charge you don't recognize, investigate it
6. **Check Family Plans**: If you're on a family plan, note your portion of cost
7. **Free Trials**: Check if any free trials converted to paid subscriptions

---

## üö® RED FLAGS (Investigate These!)

If you see these, investigate immediately:
- Charges from services you don't recognize
- Charges you thought you canceled
- Charges that seem too high
- Multiple charges from same service (might be duplicate)
- Charges from services you haven't used in months
